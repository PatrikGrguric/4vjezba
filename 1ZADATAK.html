Minecraft

Minecraft je videoigra koju je kreirao norveški švedski programer Markus Notch Persson, a kasnije razvila i izdala švedska kompanija Mojang.

Igra je izašla 2009. godine. Do danas je izašla na Windows, OS X, Linux, Java platform, Java applet, Android, iOS, Windows Phone, Xbox 360, Xbox One, Raspberry Pi, PlayStation 3, PlayStation 4, PlayStation Vita platformama.

Igru je moguće igrati u singleplayer i multiplayer modu, a prodano je preko 54 milijuna kopija igre.

Minecraft is an open world game that has no specific goals for the player to accomplish, allowing players a large amount of freedom in choosing how to play the game.
